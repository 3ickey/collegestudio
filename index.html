Topic overview

Accessibility of food recipes.

As we all know there are many delicious world food recipes out there and one doesn’t always need to go to
that specific country to taste the ultimate dish. We’re afraid to try other cultures’ food because we have
no idea what goes inside to make it and of course what it tastes like. But what if we did, what if we knew
the exact ingredients and even try to make it ourselves at home? This web app hovers over the idea of
accessibility of food recipes. I believe food is the most important thing people gather around for, whether
that’s with family or friends. And at the interpersonal level It’ll open a new possibility to form
relationships and communication between people.

The creation of this web app in this space is to simply
address the inaccessibility of food recipes and to present it with beautiful UI elements that will make the
user experience a lot better than what we experience on the web today. I believe it’ll be highly useful to people:

  • Who want to try out new recipes
  • Who are not a very good cook or not at all
  • Who want to have a general knowledge of where the recipes originate from

Potential Apps

  1. An app that provides a recipe based on specific ingredients
    • The user will input specific ingredients they have, and the app will try give them a recipe. If not, it will suggest a close match.
  2. An app that can suggest weekly complete meals with a detailed instruction on how to make it and origin of recipe.
    • By having a place for complete meals people don’t always have to search for a recipe
